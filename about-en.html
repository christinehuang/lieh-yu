About Liehyu

History

Founded in 1949, it was originally the Lieyu branch of Jincheng Junior High School. At that time, the school building department temporarily used Lieyu Shangqi Elementary School to teach in forest classes. The new school building was completed the following year, and the students were moved to the current site. The name was officially changed to "Jinmen County Lieyu National High School". At the beginning, there were only four classrooms and one office. In 1993, a three-story teaching and administrative complex was built and the school building was changed. Scale. The school has entered 53 years since its founding. The principal, Mr. Lin Yongjin, has 24 students in the seventh grade, 36 students in the eighth grade, 29 students in the ninth grade, and 29 faculty members.
